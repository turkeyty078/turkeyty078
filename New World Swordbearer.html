Swordbearer

The Sword Bearer class in New World Aeternum is an exciting choice for players seeking a blend of powerful melee combat and ranged versatility. With access to the greatsword and blunderbuss, the Sword Bearer shines in both solo play and group settings, making it a solid option for newcomers navigating the diverse landscapes of Aeternum. Lets explore why this class is an excellent starting point and how it transitions into more powerful builds for late-game content.

Skill Trees

Great Sword

You want to be fighting in onslaught stance for the most damage , using rupture for grouping and some defensive increases

1:Relentless Rush

Spin through enemies damaging them and putting you into onslaught stance

2:Rupture

When used in onslaught stance it will suck in surrounding enemies and making you un interruptible for a short time.

3:Skyward Slash

An upward slash dealing damage and rending your target reducing their armor

Skill Tree

Blunderbuss

1:Net Shot

A nice moving ability, this will allow you to kite enemies mostly in PvP. But since Blunderbuss doesnt have many long-distance skills, this is more likely an escape tool.

2:Claw Shot

Great gap-closing ability, allows you to be on top of the enemy from 15m away. This is primarily a PvP ability, as mobs dont run away from you

3:Azoth Shrapnel Blast

This ability is pretty good at very close range. It can be combined with the Unload, Containment tree ultimate, which increases the number of shots from 5 up to 9. Works great with Claw Shot, jumping to the enemy and shooting him in the face. A bomb that shoots downward if you have an Azoth Bomb skill is just more potential damage.

Attribute Points

Attribute Points for Sword Bearer

Early Game (Level 1-20)

Strength (Str): 100-150

Intelligence (Int): 25-50

Constitution (Con): 50-100

Mid Game (Level 30-50)

Strength (Str): 250-300

Intelligence (Int): 25-50

Constitution (Con): 100-150

As you progress, ramp up your Strength significantly to maximize melee damage output. Maintain a baseline of Intelligence for your blunderbuss while investing in Constitution to ensure you can withstand tougher battles

Late Game (Level 60-65)

Strength (Str): 300

Dexterity (Dex): 250-300

Intelligence (Int): 5-25

Constitution (Con): 5-50

Armor

armor recommendations, this class plays best with a LIGHT armor weight but medium also is viable for more survivability.
